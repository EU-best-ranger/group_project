Data Laws And Legislation

Over the years many different laws and legislations have been put in place to protect people within the growing indurstry. These laws and legislations are there to make sure information on people is not used or collected in a harmful, malicious manner. Most countries throughout the world have some sort of law in place to help with this.

The Data Protection Act of 2018 was put into place by the UK as their version of the GDPR. This act makes it so anyone using personal data is then put under strict princepls that need to be followed. These are called "data protection principles".

The Data Protection Act makes it so anyone responsable for your data must follow its principles, which consist of your data being used in a fair and lawful manner, your data only being used for specific purposes which are limited and used only for what is necessary. The data collected must also be accurate and up to date, being kept for no longer than its needed. The data must also be kept securely and kept protected.
